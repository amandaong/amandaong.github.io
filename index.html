Photo Editor App
December 2015
After taking CUAppDev's one-credit App Development course, I created a photo editing app that allows users to draw on photos from their camera roll. Users can adjust the opacity and size of the brush, erase, and even utilize a random color generator to pick a color. This app sparked my love for app development and encouraged me to explore my interest through other organizations on campus (i.e. iGEM and ECAFT).

Focus App
Feburary 2016
As part of the 24-hour HackBU Hackathon in Binghamton, NY, I along with three other members created a productivity app to stop users from accessing their iPhones for a set time. We utilized features such as NSTimer, Facebook SDK, NSURL, and UIPickerView to create the app.

Apple Fest Website
November 2016
As our final project for the three-credit course for Introductory Design and Programming for the Web, based on user research from eight individuals, I along with three other members redesigned Ithaca's Apple Harvest Festival website. I was responsible for the general design and code structure for the website. I implemented a sticky navigation bar with JS animations, the Schedule of Events page that utilized PHP to load events information into the table, and created the footer.
